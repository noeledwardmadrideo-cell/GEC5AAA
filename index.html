7 Cs of Communication

How to Play

You'll be tested on the 7 Cs of Communication. Each question has a 5-second timer. Answer quickly or it counts as wrong!

The 7 Cs:

1. Clarity - Is the message clear and easy to understand, or confusing?

2. Conciseness - Does it use fewer words to say the same thing effectively?

3. Correctness - Is the grammar, spelling, and usage correct?

4. Concreteness - Is it specific with clear details (who, what, when, where), or vague and unclear?

5. Completeness - Does it include all necessary information, or is something missing?

6. Courtesy - Is the message polite, respectful, and considerate?

7. Coherence - Does the information flow logically and make sense?
